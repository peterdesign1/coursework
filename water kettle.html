2. Breville Water Kettle


Overview

The aesthetics of this beautifully crafted equipment stands it out from other electric heaters. Apart from its aesthetics, it has some amazing features and functionality which was intended to give its user optimal satisfaction.

Pros

Kindly consider some of the amazing features of this masterpiece of a design.

  • Removable and rotational base, which makes it easy for repairs incase the equipment malfunctions.
  • The base also have space for cord storage. and this prevents the wires from dangling and been all over the place
  • It has a removable and cleanable rear water filter which captures dirt and lime scale particles.
  • The bottom the kettle is made of stainless steel to ensure fast and quick boiling
  • It has a smooth pour spout for easy pouring
