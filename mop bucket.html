The Mop Bucket unlike many I have seen before, has some cool distinguishing features.

  • There is a pedal attached to the bucket which helps to spin and wingle the mop causing it to drain water pretty fast. This pedal can be conveniently pulled with the foot

  • I also think the brown and red color of the mop bucket is to preserve it as against using a white colour which can get stained and makes the bucket more difficult to maintain
  • The two corners of the bucket are slightly curve and sloppy. This will make it easy to drain water out of the bucket in cautious way.
